Concept Overview遊戲概念

Welcome to Lucas's Lookout! Get ready for a fast-paced and fun game where you step into the important role of Lucas's caregiver. Little Lucas is a curious and speedy crawler, exploring his home with wide-eyed wonder. Your mission? To keep him safe from everyday household hazards that pop up along his path. It's a game of quick reflexes, sharp eyes, and learning essential baby safety tips along the way! 歡迎來到 Lucas's Lookout！準備好迎接這款快節奏又有趣的遊戲，您將扮演Lucas重要的照顧者角色。小Lucas是個好奇又爬得飛快的寶寶，正睜大眼睛探索他的家。您的任務？保護他遠離路上出現的日常家居危險。這是一款考驗快速反應、敏銳觀察力，並在過程中學習重要嬰兒安全提示的遊戲！

How to Play遊戲玩法

Keeping Lucas safe is all about quick thinking and tapping! 保護Lucas的關鍵在於快速思考和點擊！

  • Lucas Crawls: Lucas will start crawling automatically across different rooms in the house. Lucas爬行： Lucas會自動在家中不同房間爬行。
  • Hazards Appear: Watch out! Dangers will appear in his path. Some you can fix, others you need to help him avoid. 危險出現： 小心！危險會出現在他的路上。有些您可以解決，有些則需要幫助他避開。
  • Fixable Hazards: Tap directly on the Hazard (like an open outlet or small object) to make it safe. 可解決的危險： 直接點擊危險物（如未遮蓋的插座或小物件）使其安全。
  • Temporary Hazards: These disappear on their own (like a hot mug or spill). Tap directly on Lucas to distract him and change his direction to avoid these. 暫時性危險： 這些危險會自行消失（如低處的熱杯子或潑灑物）。直接點擊Lucas來分散他的注意力，改變他的方向以避開。
  • Quick Taps: You have a very short cooldown (0.3-0.5s) after tapping Lucas, so time your distractions carefully! 快速點擊： 點擊Lucas後有很短的冷卻時間（0.3-0.5秒），所以要仔細掌握分散注意力的時機！

Meet Lucas認識Lucas

Lucas is our adorable star! He's just learned to crawl and loves exploring. He's quick, curious, and heads towards interesting things... which are sometimes dangerous! His safety depends on your watchful eye and speedy taps. Can you keep up with him? Lucas是我們可愛的主角！他剛學會爬行，熱愛探索。他動作快、好奇心強，而且會朝著有趣的東西前進……而這些東西有時很危險！他的安全取決於您警惕的目光和快速的點擊。您能跟上他的步伐嗎？

Watch Out! Hazards Ahead注意！前方危險

The house is full of things Lucas might get into! Keep an eye out for: 家中充滿了Lucas可能會接觸到的東西！請留意：

  • Fixable Dangers: Uncovered electrical outlets, dropped coins or buttons, plastic bags, dangling cords, open low cabinets. (Tap these!) 可解決的危險： 未遮蓋的插座、掉落的硬幣或鈕扣、塑膠袋、懸垂的繩線、敞開的低矮櫥櫃。（點擊這些！）
  • Temporary Dangers: Hot mugs/food left low, spills, pets moving, rolling toys. (Tap Lucas to avoid these!) 暫時性危險： 低處的熱杯/食物、潑灑物、移動的寵物、滾動的玩具。（點擊Lucas避開這些！）

Test Your Reflexes測試您的反應

Things start simple, but the challenge grows! Expect Lucas to get faster, more hazards to appear, and less time to react as you progress through the levels. Stay focused! 遊戲開始時很簡單，但挑戰會逐漸增加！隨著關卡的推進，預計Lucas會爬得更快，出現更多危險，反應時間也會縮短。保持專注！
