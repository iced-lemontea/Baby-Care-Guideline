Welcome Caregivers 歡迎照顧者

Thank you for helping care for our newborn! These guidelines are designed to ensure the baby's safety and well-being at all times. 感謝您協助照顧我們的寶寶！這些守則旨在確保寶寶時刻安全與健康。

Please read all sections carefully and follow the instructions strictly. If you have any questions or are unsure about anything, always ask the parents first. 請詳細閱讀所有部分並嚴格遵守指示。如果您有任何疑問或不確定的地方，請務必先詢問父母。

Thank you for following these rules carefully to keep our baby safe. 感謝您仔細遵守這些規則以確保寶寶的安全。

  • Always support the baby's head and neck when holding or carrying. 抱寶寶時務必托住頭部和頸部，新生兒頸部無力，容易受傷。
  • Wash hands before touching the baby to prevent infections. 觸摸寶寶前請務必洗手，以防細菌感染。
  • Never shake the baby – even gentle shaking can cause brain injury. 切勿搖晃寶寶，即使是逗玩，劇烈搖晃可能導致腦部損傷。
  • No kissing on the face or hands to prevent infections. 請勿親寶寶的臉或手，避免傳染病菌。
  • Do not hold the baby when feeling sleepy or tired – always place the baby in a safe sleeping area. 感到疲倦時請勿抱寶寶，應將寶寶放在安全的睡眠區域。

Tip:提示： When picking up the baby, slide one hand under the head and neck, and the other under the bottom for full support. 抱起寶寶時，一手托住頭頸部，另一手托住臀部，給予完全支撐。

  • Baby should only have breast milk or formula – no water, honey, or solid foods before 6 months. 6 個月內只餵母乳或配方奶，嚴禁餵水、蜂蜜或任何固體食物。
  • Never prop the bottle – always hold the baby when feeding. 餵奶時請抱好寶寶，禁止讓奶瓶自行支撐，以免嗆奶。
  • Burp the baby after every feed to prevent gas and discomfort. 餵奶後務必幫寶寶拍嗝，防止脹氣不適。
  • Do not heat milk in the microwave, as it can cause uneven heating. Use a bottle warmer or warm water bath. 不可使用微波爐加熱奶瓶，容易造成燙傷風險。請使用暖奶器或溫水浸泡。
  • If the baby refuses the bottle or stops drinking, do not force-feed. 若寶寶不想喝奶，請勿強迫餵奶。

Milk Temperature:奶水溫度：
Test on your wrist – it should feel lukewarm, not hot. 滴在手腕內側測試，應感覺溫暖而不燙。

Storage:儲存：
Prepared formula should be used within 1 hour or discarded. 泡好的配方奶應在 1 小時內飲用完畢，否則應丟棄。

  • Always place the baby on their back to sleep – no side or tummy sleeping. 寶寶只能仰睡，不能趴睡或側睡，避免嬰兒猝死症（SIDS）。
  • The crib must be completely empty – no pillows, blankets, stuffed toys, or bumpers. 嬰兒床應保持空曠，不能放枕頭、棉被、玩偶或床圍。
  • No co-sleeping with adults on beds, sofas, or armchairs. 禁止與大人同床睡覺，避免壓傷或窒息風險。
  • Keep the room cool (20–22°C / 68-72°F) and smoke-free. 室內溫度應保持在 20-22°C，並確保空氣流通及無菸。

Safe Sleep Environment: 安全睡眠環境：

Firm mattress with fitted sheet only 堅實床墊，僅用床包
Sleepsack instead of blankets 使用睡袋代替棉被
Room-sharing but not bed-sharing 同房不同床
  • Wash hands before and after every diaper change. 換尿布前後務必洗手，保持衛生。
  • Wipe from front to back to prevent infections. 擦拭時，請從前往後擦拭，以防感染。
  • Change diapers frequently to prevent diaper rash. 尿布應定期更換，避免尿布疹。

Diaper Change Station Safety: 換尿布台安全：

Never leave unattended:切勿離開：
Keep one hand on baby at all times on changing table. 在換尿布台上時，務必隨時用一隻手護著寶寶。

Diaper rash care:尿布疹護理：
Use zinc oxide cream for redness. 如有紅疹可使用氧化鋅藥膏。

  • Never leave the baby unattended during bath time, not even for a second. Gather all supplies beforehand. 切勿在寶寶洗澡時離開，即使一秒鐘也不行。事先準備好所有用品。
  • Always check water temperature carefully (use wrist or elbow) before placing the baby in. It should be lukewarm. 放入寶寶前務必仔細檢查水溫（用手腕或手肘測試），應感覺溫暖而不燙。
  • Keep one hand supporting the baby at all times during the bath. 洗澡過程中，務必隨時用一隻手支撐寶寶。

Water Temp Tip:水溫提示： Aim for around 37-38°C (98.6-100.4°F). Use a bath thermometer if unsure. 目標約 37-38°C。如果不確定，請使用洗澡溫度計。

  • Never leave the baby unattended on a bed, sofa, or changing table. 請勿讓寶寶獨自躺在沙發、床上或換尿布檯上，以免跌落。
  • Keep small objects and plastic bags away to prevent choking. 遠離塑膠袋、小物件，以防寶寶誤吞窒息。
  • Avoid strong perfumes, candles, or mosquito coils – they can irritate the baby's breathing. 避免強烈香水、蠟燭、蚊香，以防影響寶寶呼吸道。
  • Check baby's temperature by feeling the back of their neck or chest; avoid overdressing. They should feel warm, not hot or sweaty. 觸摸寶寶頸背或胸口檢查體溫；避免穿著過多。應感覺溫暖，而非過熱或出汗。
  • No smoking or alcohol near the baby. 嚴禁在寶寶周圍吸菸或飲酒。
  • No direct sun exposure – protect the baby's skin. 禁止寶寶直接曝曬陽光，請適當遮蔽。
  • Do not bring the baby to the balcony. 請勿帶寶寶到陽台。
  • Do not bring the baby close to any open window – there are no window guards and we live in a high-rise. 請勿將寶寶靠近開著的窗戶 —— 我們住在高樓，且窗戶沒有防護裝置。

High-Rise Safety: 高樓安全：

Windows must remain closed when baby is in the room. Never hold baby near an open window, even if you're just looking outside. 當寶寶在房間時，窗戶必須保持關閉。切勿抱著寶寶靠近打開的窗戶，即使只是看看外面也不行。

  • The baby must always be in a rear-facing car seat (Doona) when in a vehicle. 寶寶乘車時，必須使用後向式嬰兒安全座椅 (Doona)，不可抱著寶寶乘車。
  • Do not hold the baby in your lap while in a moving car. 車輛行駛時，嚴禁將寶寶抱在懷中。
  • Make sure the car seat straps are properly secured and snug. 確保安全座椅的帶子已正確固定且服貼。
  • No heavy clothing or thick blankets in the car seat – this affects the harness fit. 請勿讓寶寶穿著厚重衣物或蓋厚毯子坐在安全座椅上，這會影響安全帶的服貼度。

Car Seat Checklist: 安全座椅檢查：

Harness straps should be at or below baby's shoulders 安全帶肩帶應位於寶寶肩膀或肩膀稍下方
Chest clip at armpit level, straps tight enough you can't pinch any slack 胸扣位於腋下高度，安全帶拉緊至無法捏起任何鬆弛部分
  • Do not shake, toss, or play roughly with the baby. 不可劇烈搖晃、拋起寶寶，可能導致腦部損傷。
  • Keep all toys clean and free of small parts. 玩具需定期清潔，避免細小零件，以防誤吞。
  • No screen time (TV, phone, tablet) before 18 months old. 18 個月前，禁止觀看電視、手機或平板電腦螢幕。

Appropriate Play: 適當的遊戲：

Newborns enjoy gentle interaction: talking, singing, showing high-contrast images. Avoid overstimulation - watch for signs like turning away or fussiness. 新生兒喜歡溫和的互動：說話、唱歌、看高對比圖案。避免過度刺激 - 注意寶寶轉開頭或煩躁的跡象。

  • No sick visitors – anyone with a cough, fever, or illness should stay away. 有感冒、發燒或咳嗽者，請勿接觸寶寶。
  • Avoid crowded places for the first few months. 避免帶寶寶去人多擁擠的地方，降低感染風險。
  • Protect the baby from direct sunlight with a hat or light cover. 用帽子或薄罩保護寶寶免受陽光直射。

Visitor Guidelines: 訪客守則：

All visitors must wash hands before touching baby 所有訪客接觸寶寶前必須洗手

No smoking near baby / Ask smokers to change clothes & wash hands 禁止在寶寶附近吸菸 / 要求吸菸者更衣洗手

No kissing baby's face or hands. Postpone visit if feeling unwell. 禁止親吻寶寶的臉或手。若感不適請延後探訪。

  • If the baby has difficulty breathing, turns blue, or chokes, seek help immediately (Call emergency services / Inform parents). 若寶寶呼吸困難、嘴唇發紫、窒息，請立即尋求醫療協助（致電緊急服務 / 通知父母）。
  • A fever over 38°C (100.4°F) requires immediate attention – inform parents right away. 寶寶體溫超過 38°C，請立即通知父母。
  • Do not give any medication without approval from the parents. 不可擅自給寶寶服用任何藥物，所有藥物須經父母同意。
  • Report any unusual symptoms (excessive crying, refusal to eat, rashes, lethargy) immediately. 若有任何異常症狀（過度哭鬧、拒食、皮疹、精神萎靡等），請立即回報。

Key Reminder: 重要提醒：

Ensure you have parent contact numbers easily accessible at all times. In any doubt or emergency, contact parents immediately. 確保您隨時可以方便地取得父母的聯絡電話。如有任何疑問或緊急情況，請立即聯絡父母。
