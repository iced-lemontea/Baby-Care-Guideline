Lucas's Lookout! Lucas's Lookout!

About The Game 關於遊戲

Uh oh! Little Lucas has just learned to crawl, and the whole house is his new playground! He's speedy, curious, and naturally drawn to interesting things... which aren't always safe. 噢噢！小 Lucas 剛學會爬行，整個家都是他的新遊樂場！他速度快、好奇心強，而且天生會被有趣的東西吸引……但這些東西不一定安全。

Lucas's Lookout is a fast-paced, reaction-based game where you take on the vital role of Lucas's caregiver. Your mission is to keep him safe from everyday household hazards as he explores different rooms. Can you keep up with his adventures? Lucas's Lookout 是一款快節奏、考驗反應的遊戲，您將扮演 Lucas 的重要照顧者。您的任務是在他探索不同房間時，保護他遠離日常的家居危險。您能跟上他的冒險步伐嗎？

Gameplay 遊戲玩法

The goal is simple but challenging: keep Lucas safe while helping him collect his milk bottles! 目標簡單但具有挑戰性：確保 Lucas 安全，同時幫助他收集奶瓶！

  1. Watch Lucas: He crawls automatically across the screen. 觀察 Lucas：他會自動在螢幕上爬行。
  2. Spot Hazards: Dangers appear in his path. Pay close attention! 發現危險：危險物品會出現在他的路徑上。請密切注意！
  3. React Quickly: Identify the hazard type: 快速反應：辨識危險類型：
    • Fixable Hazard? (Electricity Outlet, coin, plastic bag, etc.) => Double TAP THE HAZARD itself to remove it. 可修復的危險？（電源插座、硬幣、塑膠袋等）=> **快速點擊兩下危險物品本身** 以移除它。
    • Temporary Hazard? (Kitchen knife, clothes iron, etc.) => TAP LUCAS to distract him and make him change direction. (Watch out for the brief cooldown after tapping Lucas!) 暫時性危險？（菜刀、熨斗等）=> **點擊 Lucas** 來分散他的注意力，讓他改變方向。（注意點擊 Lucas 後會有短暫的冷卻時間！）
  4. Collect Milk Bottles: Guide Lucas safely towards the 5 milk bottles that appear in each level. Help him collect them all to complete the level! 收集奶瓶：安全地引導 Lucas 前往每個關卡中出現的 5 個奶瓶。幫助他全部收集完成關卡！

Features 遊戲特色

  • 5 Challenging Levels: Guide Lucas safely through the Living Room, Kitchen, Nursery, Hallway and Bathroom. 5 個挑戰性關卡：安全引導 Lucas 通過客廳、廚房、嬰兒房、走廊和浴室。
  • Varied Hazards: Learn to handle both neutralizable and temporary dangers. 多樣的危險：學習處理可移除和暫時性的危險。
  • Fast-Paced Action: Test your reflexes and observation skills. 快節奏動作：測試您的反應和觀察能力。
  • Increasing Difficulty! 難度逐漸增加！
  • Educational: Reinforces common baby safety awareness points in a fun way. 教育性：以有趣的方式加強常見的嬰兒安全意識。
  • Win Condition: Complete all 5 levels to win! 獲勝條件：完成所有 5 個關卡即可獲勝！
