Baby Safety Knowledge Check 嬰兒安全知識測驗

Question 1 問題 1

You must always support a newborn baby's head and neck when holding them. 抱新生兒時，必須時刻托住他們的頭部和頸部。

Question 2 問題 2

It is okay to give a 2-month-old baby small sips of water if they seem thirsty. 如果 2 個月大的寶寶看起來渴了，可以給他們喝少量水。

Question 3 問題 3

Babies should always be placed on their back to sleep to reduce the risk of SIDS. 為降低嬰兒猝死症(SIDS)風險，應始終讓寶寶仰睡。

Question 4 問題 4

It's safe to quickly grab something from another room while the baby is on the changing table, as long as you are fast. 只要速度夠快，可以在寶寶躺在換尿布台上時，快速去另一個房間拿東西。

Question 5 問題 5

Hands should be washed both before and after changing a baby's diaper. 換尿布前後都應該洗手。

Question 6 問題 6

Soft blankets and stuffed animals make a crib cozier and safer for a newborn. 柔軟的毯子和填充玩偶能讓嬰兒床更舒適、更安全。

Question 7 問題 7

When traveling by car, a baby must always be secured in a rear-facing car seat. 乘車時，必須始終將寶寶固定在後向式汽車安全座椅中。

Question 8 問題 8

Holding the baby near an open window in a high-rise building is safe if you have a firm grip. 只要抓緊，在高樓建築中抱著寶寶靠近打開的窗戶是安全的。

Question 9 問題 9

It is important to burp the baby after each feeding to help release trapped gas. 每次餵奶後幫寶寶拍嗝以幫助排出吞入的空氣是很重要的。

Question 10 問題 10

Kissing the baby on the face or hands is a safe way to show affection. 親吻寶寶的臉或手是表達關愛的安全方式。

Remember to always prioritize the baby's safety! 請謹記時刻將寶寶的安全放在首位！
